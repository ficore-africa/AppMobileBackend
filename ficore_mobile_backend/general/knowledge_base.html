Mastering DIICE Bookkeeping

DIICE stands for Daily Income, Inventory, Cost, and Expenses. It is designed for the busy African entrepreneur who doesn't have time for complex accounting.

  • D - Daily: Log every sale immediately using Voice Logging or manual entry.
  • I - Income: Track cash vs. digital payments to avoid "missing money" at the end of the day.
  • I - Inventory: Automate stock alerts so you never run out of top-selling items.
  • C - Cost: Track the wholesale cost of goods to see your true margin.
  • E - Expenses: Categorize daily spending (transport, fuel, utilities) for tax deductions.
Pro Tip: Use the mobile app's Voice Entry feature to log sales in real-time. Say "Sold 5 bags of rice for 50,000 naira" and FiCore does the rest.

Nigeria Tax Act 2025 Compliance

The new regulations require small businesses to keep digital records to benefit from tax exemptions and avoid penalties.

Important: Businesses under ₦100M annual revenue are tax-exempt BUT still need digital records to prove compliance.

Voice Entry: Record Transactions in 10 Seconds

Voice Entry is FiCore's fastest way to log transactions. Speak naturally in English, Hausa, or Pidgin, and our AI does the rest.

How to Use Voice Entry
Step 1: Tap the Mic Icon

On the home screen, tap the microphone icon or the "+" button and select "Voice Entry".

Step 2: Speak Your Transaction

Say what happened: "Sold 10 bags of rice for 100,000 naira" or "Bought fuel for 5,000 naira".

Step 3: Review & Confirm

FiCore shows what it understood. Check the amount, category, and description. Tap "Save".

Step 4: Done!

Your transaction is recorded, categorized, and ready for reports. Total time: 10 seconds.

Voice Entry Examples
  • English: "Sold 5 phones for 250,000 naira"
  • Hausa: "Na sayar da jakuna 10 akan naira 50,000"
  • Pidgin: "I sell 20 bag of rice for 200,000 naira"
  • Expense: "Bought fuel for 5,000 naira"
Pro Tip: Voice Entry works offline! Record transactions even without internet, and they'll sync when you're back online.
